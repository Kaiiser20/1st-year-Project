Portfolio

Arki-Q Architecture, a highly experienced and diversified, professional architectural and design firm, offers a comprehensive architectural design and management service for every development project type and scope. From residential design to development to large-scale commercial and industrial projects, with an emphasis on personalised architectural solutions for every client.
With a growing list of awards, vast project experience and technical expertise, our focus is on delivering excellent architecture work and client service of only the highest standards.
Arki-Q is a wholly owned subsidiary of TWP Holdings, which is a large firm of consulting engineers, primarily offering professional engineering services to the mining industry, but active in most fields of construction engineering. This gives exciting possibilities for the group to offer the widest range of engineering, architectural, and interior design services to a broad spectrum of clients.
